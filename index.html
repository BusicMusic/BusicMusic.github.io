About Me

Basically, I'm a computer nerd. I'm very interested in computers in general. Videogames, Programming, Figuring out how stuff works, etc. In high school I took Graphic Arts, Programming, and Electronic Engineering classes to help me find something that I was passionate about and turn it in to a career. The Graphic Arts classes taught me a general understanding of Adobe PhotoShop as well as Adobe Illustrator. While taking the Programming classes I discovered that I loved learning how to program because it taught me a general understanding of all coding that I've come across. The Electronic Engineering classes were fun, but I didn't enjoy them as much as the programming aspect of computers. That helped me narrow down my choice of a career to: Programming. I like to think that with my Electronics background has given me a basic understanding of how circuit boards work. Plus, it ties in nicely with my interest in computers.

Once I decided to focus on programming as a career choice I started looking around for colleges that were nearby and offered a solid programming degree. This lead me to ECPI University, where I recieved my Bachelores in Software Development! While working towards the degree, I took classes for HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that really sparked my interest in Web Development. I've taken the knowledge that I've gathered from those classes and created multiple websites; both for classes as 'final exams' and as personal projects that I thought would be cool to create.

You can find links to all the finished and (hopefully) bug-free websites on the column to the left! If you want to contact me, sending me a message on Twitter would be the easiest way. However, if it's about a more professional matter, then you should already have my email address. I'm not putting my email address here because I'm not comfortable just throwing that out online for everyone to see.
